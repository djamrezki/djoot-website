What is Djoot?

Djoot is a cloud-based software platform designed for law firms. It helps lawyers track time, manage cases, automate fees and retainers, and generate compliant invoices for their clients.

How it works

  • Law firms subscribe to Djoot on a monthly or yearly basis.
  • Users log in securely to manage time entries, cases, clients and invoices.
  • Billing is handled via Stripe using credit or debit cards.
